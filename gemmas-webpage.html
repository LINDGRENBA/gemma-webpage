Gemma's Webpage

Written by Brittany Lindgren

This page is a tribute to my adorable dog Gemma. Gemma is a rescue and likes to spend her day staring out the window at the neighborhood cats, barking at the dogs on the other side of our fence in the backyard and making uncomfortable eye contact with us in an attempt to procure food.

The following is a list of Gemma's current favorite toys:

  • Mr. Snake
  • Mr. Shark
  • Mr. Bear

As you can see, there is a naming theme.
